# Carisma Aesthetics — 30-Day SMM Content Angles
**Version:** v3 — Viral Rewrite

This calendar is built on one truth the research keeps proving: the aesthetics content that goes viral is never the content that explains treatments. It's the content that picks a side, tells a specific story about a specific person, and says the thing most clinics are too corporate to say. Every angle in this calendar is built around the exact 31 topics from the viral research — topics chosen because they already have an audience looking for answers. Our job is to be the practitioner honest enough to give them. The arc runs from fear-busting (Days 1–8) through mechanism education (Days 9–21) to patient decision-making (Days 22–31). Each day's punchline comes from the research and is the core of the script, not a line buried at the end. "Subtle by Design" appears once — Day 24 — as a closing line, not a branding exercise.

## Change Log: v2 → v3

### What changed and why
v2 was built around the clinic's content needs. v3 is built around the audience's actual questions. Every topic is pulled directly from the Section 5 viral research shortlist — topics with proven search demand and TikTok engagement. The angles file in v3 strips out production direction (moved to scripts file), strategic rationale paragraphs (replaced with one-line "Why this works"), and internal QC notes (resolved). The format is leaner and maps directly to what the sheet builder needs to parse. The v2 calendar had strong topics buried under clinic-speak. v3 puts the viral punchline front and centre for every day.

### Topics replaced from v2
The v2 calendar included topics from the production team's wishlist (bridal timeline, Korean glass-skin protocol, tweakment stack, male masseter, Malta cultural bilingual, consultation pricing). These are not in the viral research. They may be shootable and useful but they are not built from audience demand. v3 replaces all of them with the exact 31 topics from Section 5 — topics the audience is already searching for. If the production team wants to add Malta-specific angles, they should be added as additional posts beyond the 31, not substituted for topics with proven demand.
