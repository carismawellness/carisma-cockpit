## Critical Interaction Warnings
1. **Berberine + Semaglutide** — Never fasted. Always with food. Monitor for dizziness/hypoglycemia.
2. **D3 without K2** — Do NOT take D3 alone long-term. Arterial calcification risk.
3. **Zinc without Copper** — Do NOT take zinc >4 weeks without copper. Neurological depletion risk.
4. **Vitamin E at dose >400 IU** — Meta-analyses show increased all-cause mortality. Keep at 150 IU mixed tocopherols.
5. **CJC/Ipamorelin** — Must be 2h+ post-last meal and pre-sleep for GH pulse to work.
